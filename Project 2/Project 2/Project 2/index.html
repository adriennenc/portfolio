Breakfast Casserole Recipe

As made by my Grandma

This recipe is for the breakfast casserole that my grandma makes from time to time. The casserole is stirred, then left in the refrigerator over night. It is then put in the oven for 45 to 60 minutes.

Serving Size About 12 servings
Prep Time 24 Hours
Cook Time 45 to 60 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • 6 slices of cubed bread
  • 1 cup of sharp Cheddar cheese, graded or shredded
  • 1 tablespoon of salt (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of dry mustard
  • 1 pound or hot or mild sausage with sage, browned and drained
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Mushrooms, green, peppers, onions, diced (optional)

Directions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in large bowl
  2. Pour into a 9*13 inch ungreased baking dish
  3. Cover and let stand in refrigerator overnight
  4. In the morning, remove the cover
  5. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 350 degrees for 45 to 60 minutes (depending on oven) until brown
  6. Cut and serve
